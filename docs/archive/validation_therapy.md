## Overall Assessment

**The Therapy theme is fragile.** Only 1 keyword clears the KEEP bar, and even with the 2 REVIEW keywords, total volume is only 56 hits across 3.26M posts. The core problem is:

1. **Generic therapy words** ("therapist," "therapy," "session") are structurally ambiguous in companion subs because users frequently discuss their real human therapists alongside their AI use.
2. **Specific AI-therapy phrases** ("ai therapist," "ai therapy") have excellent precision but low volume.
3. **Complaint phrasing** ("like a therapist") captures the wrong discourse — frustration with AI behavior, not therapeutic use.

**If Walker keeps only "ai therapist"**: Clean signal but very thin (28 hits). Trend line will be sparse and noisy.

**If Walker keeps "ai therapist" + "ai therapy" + "free therapy"**: 56 hits with ~80%+ combined relevance. Still thin but more viable for trend detection.

**Possible additions to explore**: "better than my therapist" and "replaced my therapist" have perfect face validity but virtually zero volume. "cheaper than therapy" and "can't afford therapy" are thematically dead-on but also very low volume. These might grow over time as the phenomenon becomes more common.
